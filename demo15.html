Jaipur - Pushkar Tour 4.9

Code: #JT-006
Duration: 2 Nights / 3 Days
Cost: ₹ 15,920/- per person Tax inclusive


Description

Pushkar is one of the renowned holy places of India, where divinity meets with vibrant culture. Tiny but dynamic, Pushkar is a divine city that you truly cannot afford to miss. If you want yourself to familiarize with true mysticism of this city, contact Jaipur Traveling. Choose a package offered by us or let us know your preferences. We will offer you the most reasonable and customized Pushkar tour package.

Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

  • Day : 1 ARRIVAL IN JAIPUR

    We will Pick you up from Jaipur Airport / Railway Station by placard carrying driver and transfer to Hotel. Later visit the Pink City, City Palace, the ancient Observatory and Local Market. Overnight Jaipur

    Places to be visited Jaipur:     City Palace, Jantar Mnatar, Hawa Mahal & Local market

  • Day : 2 JAIPUR - AJMER / PUSHKAR - JAIPUR

    Drive to Pushkar / Ajmer for a day return trip. Ajmer was founded by the Chauhan ruler, Aiaipal, during the 11th century & is dominated by the Taragarh Fort built by Prithviraj Chauhan. In Pushkar, visit the holy Lake and also the famous Brahma Temple. Pushkar is one of the holiest places for Hindus. Later drive back to Jaipur. Overnight Jaipur.

    Places to be visited in Jaipur:     Ajmer Dargah, Brahma temple, Pushkar Lake & Local Market.

  • Day : 3 DEPARTURE FROM JAIPUR

    After breakfast, visit the magnificent Amber Palace. See the Largest Cannon on Wheels in the World, and old cannon foundry at Jaigarh. Later drive to Jaipur airport for your home destination. (Tour ends here with sweet memories)

    Places to be visited in Jaipur:     Amber Fort, Sheesh Mahal, Jaigarh Fort, Jal Mahal & Jagat Siromani temple.
