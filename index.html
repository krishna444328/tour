    UDAIPUR GUIDE

    City of lakes, Udaipur is set around a series of artificial lakes and is known for its lavish royal residences. Nestled in the south of Rajasthan its famous for Lake Pichola and white-marble Lake Palace.

    JAISALMER GUIDE

    Also know as the Golden city, Jaisalmer is a princely state in the western Indian state of Rajasthan and in the heart of the Thar desert. it's distinguished by its yellow sandstone architecture.
