Golden Triangle Tour (Jaipur – Agra – Delhi OR Delhi – Agra – Jaipur)

If travel is on your mind and you want to experience the cultural heartbeat of India, the Golden Triangle Tour is your perfect pick! With flexible routing—Jaipur → Agra → Delhi or Delhi → Agra → Jaipur—this tour covers three of India’s most iconic cities. Each destination offers majestic architecture, vibrant bazaars, royal heritage, and timeless Mughal marvels. With Shreekharni Tourism, expect a seamless journey, friendly staff, and top-notch arrangements from start to finish.

Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

  • DAY 01: ARRIVAL IN JAIPUR 

    1. Warm welcome at Jaipur Airport/Railway Station by our representative.
    2. Transfer to hotel and check-in.
    3. Afternoon city tour:
    Albert Hall Museum
    Birla Auditorium
    Local Markets for shopping
    4.Overnight stay in Jaipur.

  • DAY 02: JAIPUR CITY TOUR
    1. After breakfast, a full-day Pink City tour: Amber Fort – the ancient capital of the state Sheesh Mahal (Hall of Mirrors) City Palace, Jantar Mantar (Observatory) Jal Mahal (photo stop) Drive past Hawa Mahal, explore Ram Niwas Garden Wander through colorful local bazaars 2.Overnight stay in Jaipur. 

  • DAY 03: AGRA → DELHI (230 KMS / 5 HRS)
    1. Early morning sunrise visit to Taj Mahal (if not visited day before).
    2. Explore Red Fort and Agra’s local markets.
    3. Proceed to Delhi. On arrival, check-in at hotel.
    4. Overnight stay in Delhi.

  • DAY 04: AGRA - DELHI (230 KMS / 5 HRS)
    Morning visit World famous Love monument Taj mahal at sun rise, Red Fort and drive to Delhi. Upon arrival in Delhi check inti hotel. Rest of the day free. Overnight Delhi.

    Place to visit in Agra: World Famous Taj Mahal, Red Fort & Local Market.
