Ranthambhore & Jaipur Tour Details

Ranthambhore is among most sought-after travel destinations by adventure seekers. A true amalgamation of natural beauty and wildlife diversity, this city has everything to provide you with a thrilling experience. So, if you desire to take a break from your monotonous life, plan a trip to Ranthambhore to dive into an enthralling world by contacting Jaipur Traveling. We have customized Ranthambhore Tour packages. We assure you that it will be the most memorable experience of Ranthambhore Safari.

Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

  • DAY 1: ARRIVAL IN JAIPUR

    We will Pick you up from Jaipur Airport / Railway Station by placard carrying driver and transfer to Hotel. Later visit the Pink City, City Palace, the ancient Observatory and Local Market. Overnight Jaipur.

    Places to visit in Jaipur:     City Palace, Jantar Manatar, Hawa Mahal & Shopping at Local Market.

     

  • DAY 2: JAIPUR - RANTHAMBHORE (180 KMS / 4 HRS)
    Departure after breakfast for Ranthambhore. Upon arrival check into the hotel. Later proceed to safari in national park. Evening free at your leisure. Overnight stay at hotel.

    Places to visit in Ranthambhore: Tiger Safari
     

  • DAY 3: RANTHAMBHORE - JAIPUR (180 KMS / 4 HRS)
    Morning do the second visit to national park. After breakfast drive back to Jaipur. Rest of the at your leisure. Overnight Jaipur.

    Places to visit in Ranthambhore: Tiger Safari

  • DAY 4: DEPARTURE FROM JAIPUR
    Morning tour of Jaipur. Visit the Amber Fort - the ancient capital of the State. Visit the Sheesh Mahal or the Hall of Victory glittering with mirrors. . Later drive back to Jaipur airport / Train station for your home destination. (Tour Ends here)

    Places to visit in Jaipur: Amber Fort, Sheesh Mahal, Jagat Siromani temple, Jal Mahal & shopping. (Tour ends here with sweet memories)
