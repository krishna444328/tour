Jaipur & Udaipur Tour Details

Udaipur is among the most renowned tourist destinations of Rajasthan. Famous for beautiful lakes, captivating landscape, and royal weddings, take a close look at this city with Udaipur Tour package. If you are still unaware about the charisma possessed by captivating Lake City, contact Jaipur Traveling and we will make the tour worth from every single aspect. We assure you most memorable tour experience by providing you top-notch amenities that too at an affordable price. Experience the true meaning of hospitality by choosing from diverse Udaipur Tour packages.

Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

  • DAY 1: ARRIVAL IN JAIPUR

    We will Pick you up from Jaipur Airport / Railway Station by placard carrying driver and transfer to Hotel. Afternoon visit Amber Fort, Birla Mandir, Albert Hall & Local Market. Overnight Jaipur.

    Places to visit in Jaipur: Amber Fort, Birla Mandir, Albert Hall & Shopping at Local Market.

  • DAY 2: JAIPUR SIGHTSEEING
    Morning tour of Jaipur. Jaipur Rajasthan was given a colour coat of pink a century ago in honour of a visiting Prince. Built by Maharaja Jai Singh, the notable astronomer, this city is 260 years old. Visit Maharaja's City Palace, & the Observatory . Drive past Hawa Mahal & through the pink rose residential & business areas. Afternoon free. Overnight Jaipur.

    Places to visit in Jaipur: Hawa mahal, City Palace, Jantar Mantar, Jal Mahal & Local Market.
     

  • JAIPUR - PUSHKAR - UDAIPUR (450 KMS / 7 HRS)
    Morning drive to Udaipur en route visiting Pushkar. In Pushkar, visit the holy Lake and also the famous Brahma Temple. Pushkar is one of the holiest places for Hindus. Upon arrival check in at the hotel. Rest of the day at your leisure. Evening enjoy a romantic Boat ride at Lake Pichhola visiting Jag Mandir. Overnight Udaipur.

    Places to visit in Pushkar: Brahma Temple, Pushkar Lake & Boat Ride at Lake Pichhola
     

  • DAY 4: UDAIPUR SIGHTSEEING
    Morning tour of Udaipur. Visit the City Palace, Jagdish Temple, drive around Fateh Sagar Lake, Sahelion ki Bari and Bhartiya Lok Kala Mandir. Rest of the day your leisure. Overnight Udaipur.

    Places to visit in Udaipur: City Palace, Jagdish temple, Saheliyon ki Bari, Lok kala Mandir & Local Market.
     

  • DAY 5: DEPARTURE FROM UDAIPUR OR JAIPUR
    After breakfast drive to Udaipur Airport / Tran station or Jaipur airport / Train station for your home destination. (Tour ends here with sweet memories)
