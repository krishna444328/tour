Jaipur & Udaipur Tour Details

Udaipur, the City of Lakes, is one of the most enchanting destinations in Rajasthan, known for its scenic beauty, royal architecture, and romantic vibes. Paired with the vibrant charm of Jaipur, this tour offers a perfect blend of heritage, culture, and leisure. With Shreekharni Tourism, embark on a journey where comfort meets tradition. Enjoy personalized service, handpicked stays, and immersive sightseeing—all at an affordable price.

Detailed Day-Wise Itinerary

  • DAY 1: ARRIVAL IN JAIPUR

    Arrival at Jaipur Airport / Railway Station where our placard-carrying driver will receive and transfer you to your hotel. In the afternoon, visit the majestic Amber Fort, the spiritual Birla Mandir, the historical Albert Hall Museum, and stroll through the local markets. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

    Places to Visit: Amber Fort, Birla Mandir, Albert Hall, Local Market

  • DAY 2:Jaipur Sightseeing
    Discover the royal city of Jaipur. Visit the City Palace, Jantar Mantar (Observatory), and drive past the iconic Hawa Mahal and Jal Mahal. Learn about Jaipur’s rich heritage as you explore its pink-hued old city. Afternoon is free for leisure or shopping. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

    Places to Visit: Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Jantar Mantar, Jal Mahal, Local Market
     

  • DAY 3: Jaipur – Pushkar – Udaipur (450 km / 7 hrs)
    Drive to Udaipur, with a scenic stop at the sacred town of Pushkar. Visit the revered Brahma Temple and the holy Pushkar Lake. Upon arrival in Udaipur, check-in to the hotel. In the evening, enjoy a serene boat ride on Lake Pichhola, visiting Jag Mandir. Overnight stay in Udaipur.

    Places to Visit: Pushkar Lake, Brahma Temple, Boat Ride at Lake Pichhola Lake Pichhola
     

  • DAY 4: Udaipur Sightseeing
    After breakfast, explore the royal city of Udaipur. Visit the City Palace, Jagdish Temple, Fateh Sagar Lake, Saheliyon ki Bari, and the Bhartiya Lok Kala Mandir. Afternoon free for relaxation or shopping. Overnight stay in Udaipur.

    Places to Visit: City Palace, Jagdish Temple, Fateh Sagar Lake, Saheliyon ki Bari, Lok Kala Mandir
     

  • DAY 5: Departure from Udaipur or Jaipur
    After breakfast, transfer to Udaipur Airport/Railway Station or return drive to Jaipur Airport/Railway Station for your journey back home. Tour ends with royal memories and smiles.
