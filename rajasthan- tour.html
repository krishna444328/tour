Rajasthan Royal Trail – Jaipur, Jodhpur, Jaisalmer & Udaipur Tour

From majestic forts to golden deserts and romantic lakes, this tour offers a journey through the most iconic and vibrant cities of Rajasthan – Jaipur, Jodhpur, Jaisalmer, and Udaipur. Whether it's camel rides in the sand dunes, royal palaces, or cultural charm, this itinerary promises an unforgettable experience.

Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

  • DAY 1: ARRIVAL IN JAIPUR  

    Pickup from Jaipur Airport/Railway Station by our representative. Visit the magnificent: . Hawa Mahal – The Palace of Winds . City Palace – Royal residence and museum . Jantar Mantar – Historic astronomical observatory . Jal Mahal (Drive past) – Water palace in the middle of Man Sagar Lake . Local Market – Explore Rajasthani textiles, handicrafts & street snacks Overnight stay in Jaipur.

  • DAY 2: JAIPUR - JODHPUR (350 KMS / 6 HRS)
    After breakfast, drive to the Blue City – Jodhpur. On arrival, explore: . Mehrangarh Fort – One of India’s most stunning forts . Jaswant Thada – White marble cenotaph . Umaid Bhawan Palace – Royal residence & museum . Jodhpur Bazaars – Famous for antiques, fabrics, and spices Overnight stay in Jodhpur.

  • DAY 3: JODHPUR - JAISALMER (330 KMS / 5 HRS)
    Depart for Jaisalmer after breakfast. En route, visit: . Osian Jain Temples – Beautifully carved ancient temples in the Thar Desert In the evening, explore: . Gadisar Lake – Scenic artificial lake surrounded by temples . Local Market – Shop for puppets, jewelry, and textiles Overnight stay in Jaisalmer.  

  • DAY 4: JAISALMER SIGHTSEEING
    Post breakfast, visit: . Jaisalmer Fort (Sonar Quila) – Golden sandstone marvel . Ancient Havelis – Like Patwon Ki Haveli & Salim Singh Ki Haveli . Jain Temples – Located inside the fort . Gadisar Lake – Peaceful water body surrounded by shrines In the evening, drive to Sam Sand Dunes: . Camel Ride & Sunset View . Rajasthani Cultural Show & Folk Dance . Enjoy a royal desert experience in a Swiss Tent Camp Overnight in Jaisalmer (Swiss Tent).  

  • DAY 5: JAISALMER - RANAKPUR - UDAIPUR (490 KMS / 7 HRS)
    . Morning drive to Udaipur. . En route stop at Ranakpur Jain Temples – Renowned for intricate marble carvings. . On arrival in Udaipur, enjoy a romantic Boat Ride on Lake Pichhola, visiting Jag Mandir Palace. Overnight stay in Udaipur.
