Overview

Bhangarh boasts the historical ruins and is also well known as the most haunted place. Situated at the distance of 85 kms from Jaipur, this place will add thrill and excitement to the journey. At this personalized tour, you get to know about the history of Bhangarh and other two famous places known as Chand Baoli stepwell and Harshat Mata temple situated in Abhaneri located at the distance of 15 kms from Bhangarh. In the fort of Bhangarh, after sunset and before sunrise entry is restricted. The temple of Lord Shiva, Lord Hanuman and Dev Narayan is there at the entrance of Bhangarh fort. Another famous location of the tour is Abhaneri that is well known for two historical places: Harshat Mata temple and Chand Baoli stepwell. The stepwell is famous being one of the deepest and largest step wells in India. These tank gardens were constructed thousand years ago have exclusive geometrical configuration.
