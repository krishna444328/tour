Jaipur Tour Package 4.9

Code: #JT-001
Duration: 2 Nights / 3 Days
Cost: ₹10,452/- per person Tax inclusive

Description

Discover Jaipur in its simplest form. This tour visits Jaipur's most iconic palaces, places of historical interest and local markets. Some of the places you will visit include Albert hall, Birla Mandir, City Palace, Jantar Manatar, Hawa Mahal..

Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

  • Day : 1 ARRIVAL IN JAIPUR

    We will Pick you up from Jaipur Airport / Railway Station by placard carrying driver and transfer to Hotel. Afternoon proceed for city tour visiting Albert Hall, Birla Mandir. Overnight Jaipur.
    Places to be visited Jaipur: Albert hall & Birla Mandir.

  • Day : 2 JAIPUR CITY TOUR

    Morning tour of Jaipur. Jaipur Rajasthan was given a colour coat of pink a century ago in honour of a visiting Prince. Built by Maharaja Jai Singh, the notable astronomer, this city is 260 years old. Visit Maharaja's City Palace, & the Observatory . Drive past Hawa Mahal & through the pink rose residential & business areas. Afternoon free. Overnight Jaipur.
    Places to be visited in Jaipur: City Palace, Jantar Manatar, Hawa Mahal & Local Market

  • Day : 3 DEPARTURE FROM JAIPUR

    Morning tour of Jaipur. Visit the Amber Fort - the ancient capital of the State. Visit the Sheesh Mahal or the Hall of Victory glittering with mirrors. . Later drive back to Jaipur airport / Train station for your home destination. (Tour ends here with sweet memories)
    Places to be visited in Jaipur: Amber Fort, Glass Palace & Jagat Siromani Temple.
