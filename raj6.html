Jaipur, Jodhpur, Jaisalmer and Udaipur Tour Details

Every single city of Rajasthan possesses distinctive charm and Jaisalmer is no exception. This city is renowned for majestic forts, thrilling elephant rides and captivating sand dunes. If you are yet to experience the enthralling beauty of this lovely city, you must glance at diverse Jaisalmer Rajasthan tour packages, offered by Jaipur Traveling. We are also competent in offering customized tour packages. So, feel free to contact us with your travel plan and we will be glad to design best-customized package for you.

Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

  • DAY 1: ARRIVAL IN JAIPUR  

    We will Pick you up from Jaipur Airport / Railway Station by placard carrying driver and transfer to Hotel. Later visit the Pink City, City Palace, the ancient Observatory and Local Market. Overnight Jaipur.

    Places to visit in Jaipur: Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Jantar Mantar, Jal Mahal & Local Market.

  • DAY 2: JAIPUR - JODHPUR (350 KMS / 6 HRS)
    After breakfast drive to Jodhpur. Up on arrival check in to hotel. Later city tour of Jodhpur visit the Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada and Bazaars of Jodhpur. Later visit the Umaid Bhawan Palace. Overnight at Jodhpur.

    Places to visit in Jodhpur: Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada, Umaid Bhawan Garden & Local Market.
     

  • DAY 3: JODHPUR - JAISALMER (330 KMS / 5 HRS)
    After breakfast in the morning drive to Jaisalmer on the way stop at Ossian to visit the Jain Temple. On arrival check in to hotel. Rest of the day at Leisure. Evening visit man-made Gadisar Lake (Jaisalmer's only source of water for long periods) and the royal Cenotaphs at Bada Bagh. Overnight at Jaisalmer.

    Places to visit on the way: Osian Temple, Gadisar lake and Bada Bagh in Jaisalmer.
     

  • DAY 4: JAISALMER SIGHTSEEING & DESERT EXPERIENCE
    After breakfast visit the Jaisalmer Fort, Carved Patwon Ki Haveli, Nathmal Ki Haveli and Salim Singh Ki Haveli. Afternoon  drive to an unique experience at Jaisalmer Sand Dunes. Do a Romatic Camel Ride at the time of Sunset. Enjoy Gala Dinner at Sand Dunes Resort with Rajasthani Cultural Show, Dance and Music. Overnight stay in Swiss Tents at Jaisalmer Deserts Area.

    Places to visit in Jaisalmer: Ancient Havelis, Jaisalmer Fort, Sand Dunes on Camel Back and Rajasthani Cultural Show with Delicious Local Dinner.
     

  • DAY 5: JAISALMER - RANAKPUR - UDAIPUR (490 KMS / 7 HRS)
    After breakfast drive to Udaipur en route visiting Ranakpur Jain Temples. Upon arrival in Udaipur Check in to the hotel. Rest of the day at your leisure. Evening enjoy a Romantic Boat Ride at Lake Pichhola visiting Jag Mandir. Overnight Udaipur.

    Places to visit in Udaipur: Lake Pichhola, Jag Mandir & Local Market.
     

  • DAY 6: UDAIPUR SIGHTSEEING
    After breakfast at hotel proceed for Udaipur City Tour. Visit the City Palace, Jagdish Temple, drive around Fateh Sagar Lake, Sahelion ki Bari and Bhartiya Lok Kala Mandir. Rest of the day at leisure. Overnight Udaipur.

    Places to visit in Udaipur: City Palace, Fateh Sagar Lake, Shaheliyon ki Bari, Jagdish Temple & Bhartiya Lok Kala Mandal.
     

  • DAY 7: UDAIPUR - PUSHKAR - JAIPUR (450 KMS / 7 HRS)
    After breakfast drive to Jaipur en route visiting Pushkar. In Pushkar, Visit the Holy Pushkar Lake and also the Famous Brahma Temple and Local Market. Pushkar is one of the holiest places for Hindus. Overnight Jaipur.

    Places to visit in Pushkar: Pushkar Lake, Brahma Temple & Beautiful Local Market.
     

  • DAY 8: DEPARTURE FROM JAIPUR
    After Breakfast proceed for Jaipur City Tour. Visit the Amber Fort - the ancient capital of the State. Visit the Sheesh Mahal or the Hall of Victory glittering with mirrors and Panna Meena Ka Kund. Later drive back to Jaipur Airport / Train station for your home destination. (Tour ends here with sweet memories)

    Places to visit in Jaipur: Amber Fort & Palace, Sheesh Mahal and Panna Meena Ka Kund.
